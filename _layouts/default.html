More Than A Brand

Rollmaster Kush represents a lifestyle that embraces a wide range of strains of cannabis, various growing methods, and nutrient products. RMK is my official grow log and blog related to my commerical and residential cannabis growing adventures. Continue to scroll to read my reviews, personal grow logs, various articles covering cannabis related topics and much more.

Venture through my content and you will many different techniques being touted and being explored including supercropping, various levels of defoliation, topping, transplanting, and more. Beyond growing techniques, RMK also offers reviews on indoor gardening equipment and nutrients that may benefit your garden. Often times as individuals we look for the gauranteed solution, but there is not always a one-size-fits-all for solutions and the same can be said for all things cannabis. Join me on our journey through the garden!
