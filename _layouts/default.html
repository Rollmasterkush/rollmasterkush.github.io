More Than A Brand

Rollmaster Kush represents a lifestyle that is more than one strain of cannabis, a particular growing method, or advertiser for a certain brand of products. Instead, RMK stands to be an inclusive source for everything cannabis related. Here you can find honest product reviews, personal grow logs, various articles covering cannabis related topics and much more. Don't hesitate to search for your favorite products or research something new.

Venture through our content and you will many different techniques being touted and being explored including supercropping, various levels of defoliation, topping, transplanting, and more. Beyond growing techniques, RMK also offers reviews on indoor and outdoor gardening equipment and nutrients that may benefit your garden. Often times as individuals we look for the gauranteed solution, but there is not always a one-size-fits-all for solutions and the same can be said for all things cannabis. Join us on our journey through the garden!

Passionate Growing

Passionate growing is about doing what you love and loving what you do and here at RMK we love what we do. The first concern for every self-aware cannabis consumer is what ingredients are used to grow cannabis and is it fit for consumption. RMK lauds nutrients and sprays that are safe for not only the cannabis consumer, but the planet as well.
