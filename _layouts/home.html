More Than A Brand

Rolling Mountain Kush represents a cannabis horticulture lifestyle that embraces a wide range of genetics, various growing methods, and nutrient products. Continue reading to find a wealth of information related to growing quality cannabis.

I won't shy away from any particular method and I experiment with many different techniques including supercropping, various levels of defoliation, topping, transplanting, foliar sprays, pest management, and more. Often times as cannabis cultivators we look for the gauranteed solution, but there is never a one-size-fits-all. You must adapt to your environment and overcome obstacles that are somewhat unique to your grow.
