More Than A Brand

Rolling Mountain Kush represents a cannabis horticulture lifestyle that embraces a wide range of genetics, various growing methods, and nutrient products. Continue reading to find a wealth of information related to growing quality cannabis and the topics associated with growing cannabis in either residential or commercial settings.

We are all about experimentation and innovation. We research and experiment with many different techniques for cannabis cultivation including supercropping, various levels of defoliation, topping, transplanting, foliar sprays, and pest management on all sorts of cannabis varieties. Often times as cannabis cultivators we look for the gauranteed solution, but there is never a one-size-fits-all. We must adapt to our environment and overcome obstacles that are somewhat unique to the grow.
