Cannabis Branding

Rolling Mountain Kush represents a cannabis horticulture lifestyle that embraces a wide range of genetics, various growing methods, and nutrient products. There are various uses for different types of products that depend on several factors for determining which is best. From liquid nutrients, dry amendments, coco, rockwool, and soil there are plenty of different methods to grow excellent bud.

It is important to experiment with several techniques for cannabis cultivation including training, various levels of defoliation, topping, transplanting, foliar sprays, laying trellis, and overall pest management on a variety of cannabis strains. Often times as cannabis cultivators we look for the gauranteed solution, but we must adapt to our environment and overcome obstacles that are somewhat unique to the grow environment.
